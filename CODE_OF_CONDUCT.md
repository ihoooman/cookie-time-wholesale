# Code of conduct

## Our standard

We want collaboration around Cookie Time Wholesale to be respectful, useful,
and welcoming. Participants are expected to communicate constructively, accept
technical feedback in good faith, and focus discussion on the work.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- showing empathy and patience;
- giving specific, actionable feedback;
- respecting different experience levels and perspectives;
- acknowledging mistakes and correcting them;
- protecting private customer and contributor information.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate
disruption, and publishing another person's private information are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and other
contributions that violate this standard. They may also temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be
reviewed fairly and kept confidential to the extent possible.
